Adam Cole Injury Update

# Adam Cole Injury Update: Matt Jackson Provides Positive News on Young Bucks' Partner

Matt Jackson of The Young Bucks has spoken out about his tag team partner Adam Cole, who continues his recovery from a serious health issue that has kept him sidelined from competition.

Cole has been out of action since July 2025, when he announced that he was stepping away from wrestling to deal with health concerns. It was later reported that Cole was dealing with concussion issues, a problem apparently serious enough to warrant his extended absence from the ring.

During his time away, Cole was forced to vacate the TNT Championship at AEW All In, marking another significant setback in his recovery timeline. Despite the lack of frequent public updates on his condition, Jackson recently broke his silence to discuss his partner's progress.

Jackson expressed his affection for Cole while addressing the injury situation. "Adam Cole is the sweetest man, I miss him so much," Jackson said, before offering an encouraging assessment of Cole's recovery. According to Jackson, Cole continues to get better every day.

The positive remarks from Jackson suggest that while Cole's recovery has been gradual, there is genuine progress being made behind the scenes. With concussion issues being notoriously difficult to predict and treat, the day-by-day improvement Jackson referenced indicates Cole is taking the necessary steps to ensure a full and safe return to in-ring competition when he is medically cleared to do so.
