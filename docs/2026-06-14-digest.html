AEW Survival of the Fittest TBS Title Match Qualifiers

The first two competitors are now officially locked in for AEW's Survival of the Fittest championship match. This six-woman elimination match will provide an exciting, unpredictable avenue for crowning the next TBS Champion.

With four qualifying spots still available, AEW has begun building significant intrigue around the match's full field. Several notable women's wrestlers have already publicly expressed their interest in competing for the vacant title. Among those who have thrown their names into consideration are Kris Statlander, Mina Shirakawa, and Harley Cameron. As additional qualifiers emerge over the coming weeks, the Survival of the Fittest match is poised to become one of AEW's most compelling championship contests.
